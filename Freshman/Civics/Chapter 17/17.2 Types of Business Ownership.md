# 17.2 Types of Business Ownership
## A. Single Proprietor
- Easy to start
- Keeps all of profit
- Makes all decisions
- Does all the work
- Must solve all problems
- Responsible for all debts and lawsuits
- When sued, might have to give up businesses and possessions if case is loss

- Shares workload
- Splits profit and debts
